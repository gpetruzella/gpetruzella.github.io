MCLA-Drury Early College

Drury High School (Drury) and the Massachusetts College of Liberal Arts (MCLA) have partnered to offer the Early College at Drury-MCLA program. We aim for every Drury student to earn 30+ college credits while still in high school...at no expense.

Register today! Contact us.

Equity of access to all students is essential to the Early College program, and fundamental to the priorities of Drury and MCLA, as we support the expansion of Early College courses to all students.
