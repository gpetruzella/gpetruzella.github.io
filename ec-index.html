Drury-MCLA: Berkshire Partners for Free College

Drury High School and the Massachusetts College of Liberal Arts (MCLA) are partnering to launch the Early College at Drury-MCLA program, empowering all Drury High School students to take college classes for free.

We aim for every Drury student to earn 30 free college credits while still in high school. The Early College at Drury-MCLA program ensures that all learners have the supportive structure they need to earn transferable college credits.

Students can earn college credit at a pace that's comfortable and customizable.

Whatever you decide to do after graduation, your college experience will help you open new doors and discover new passions.

Mission Statement

The goal of Early College is to make higher education possible for students who have traditionally been underrepresented in institutions of higher learning. The mission of our program is to provide all students with an equal opportunity to complete a college degree regardless of race, financial standing, or learning ability. We expect all Drury students to be prepared to successfully complete college coursework in one of our three specialized Early College pathways (Health Sciences, Education, and Computer Science) or in the Early College Liberal Arts pathway. All students in the North Adams Public Schools, starting with the graduating Class of 2027, will take 30+ college credits free prior to graduation!
